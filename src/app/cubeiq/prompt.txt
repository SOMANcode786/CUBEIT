**Get noticed → build trust → attract customers → convert them → reconnect → grow repeatedly.**

**Attract → Engage → Convert → Reconnect → Retain → Scale**

* What CubeIQ does
* Why their business may need it
* How CubeIQ helps
* What services are available
* What happens when they work with us
* Why CubeIQ is different from a normal marketing agency

The copy should feel confident, clear, premium and intelligent.

Avoid excessive terminology such as ROAS, funnels, attribution, CTR, CRM architecture, etc. unless briefly explained in plain language.

## PERFORMANCE

The page must remain production-grade.

* Lazy-load heavy Three.js functionality where appropriate.
* Avoid unnecessarily large textures.
* Optimize DPR.
* Dispose Three.js resources correctly.
* Avoid permanent expensive render loops where unnecessary.
* Pause/reduce rendering outside the viewport when possible.
* Avoid excessive shaders and particles.
* Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
* Provide a graceful non-WebGL/reduced-motion experience.
* Keep mobile GPU limitations in mind.
* Avoid scroll-jacking.

Use native scrolling with smooth motion layered on top.

Do not make the page difficult to navigate.
